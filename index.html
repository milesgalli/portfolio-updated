About Me

Originally born in Zimbabwe, lived in Botswana and relocated to Australia with my family in 2004. From a young age, I was able to understand how fortunate I am to live in a country like Australia. This motivated me to make the most of every opportunity that came my way. Being an immigrant in Australia, sport become a way for me to assimilate into Australian culture. Playing team sports such as rugby and cricket formed the basis of my understandings of team work and allowed me to experience leadership, which I thrived on.

My passion for business began at an early age whilst experiencing shopping in the major food chain supermarkets. Later, I began reading books relating to business and self made success stories. This interest drove me to begin my studies in the field of Marketing and Management. As I progressed further in my readings about Silicon Valley. My interest for technology began to grow, immersing myself with podcasts, books and latest reports. I am a lover of the outdoors in general, devoting my time to surfing, free diving and fishing in the ocean. I love to travel with my friends. Some of my favourite places to travel include, Indonesia, Spain, Thailand, Greece and Croatia.
